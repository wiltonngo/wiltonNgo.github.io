RockBuster DVD Video Rental- SQL Project

Background

RockBuster LLC is a movie rental company that used to have stores around the world. Facing stiff competition from streaming services such as Netflix and Amazon Prime, the rockbuster Stealth management team is planning to use its existing movie licenses to launch online video rental services to stay competitive.



Objective

Perform an analyis to help with the launch strategy for the new online video service and to answer management business questions.



Key Questions and Objectives

  • What was the average Rental Duration for all Videos?
